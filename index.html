HOW IT WORKS
THE TECHNOLOGY
Step 1 — Biometric Capture
MediaPipe maps 478 facial landmarks or 33 body pose points in real-time. The guided scan walks you through poses to capture every angle.

Step 2 — Mathematical Analysis
Raw landmark coordinates are used to compute jaw angle, canthal tilt, facial symmetry, shoulder-to-hip ratio, posture angle, and more. These are objective measurements.

Step 3 — AI Scoring
Reference photos + biometric measurements are sent to Claude AI for holistic assessment. The AI scores individual traits and provides a composite score calibrated to a realistic bell curve.

Step 4 — Results
You get a detailed breakdown of every trait, your biometric measurements, AI insights, and an overall tier ranking. Share your score card or compete on the leaderboard.
TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
• Lighting is everything — face a window or use bright, even lighting
• Camera at eye level for face scans
• Prop phone 6ft away for body scans (lean against a wall)
• Plain background helps the AI focus on you
• Shirtless body scans get more accurate muscle/BF scores
• Follow the voice prompts — each pose captures different data
