HOW IT WORKS
MEET MAXX
I'm Maxx, your AI biometric scanner. I use computer vision to map your face and body in real-time, then score your attractiveness on a 1-10 scale using real measurement data.

Here's what happens when you scan:
1
BIOMETRIC CAPTURE
Face: 478 landmarks are tracked in real-time using MediaPipe AI. That green mesh you see? Each point is a precise coordinate on your face. I guide you through 8 poses — front, profiles, smile, chin angles — to map your complete facial geometry.
Body: 33 pose landmarks track your skeleton. I draw a full wireframe mesh over your body, measure shoulder-to-hip ratio live on screen, and capture your physique from multiple angles.
2
MATHEMATICAL ANALYSIS
From those landmarks, I compute objective measurements: jaw angle in degrees, canthal tilt, bilateral symmetry ratio, facial thirds proportions, shoulder-to-hip ratio, posture angle. These aren't opinions — they're math. Hundreds of measurements averaged across every pose with outlier filtering.
3
AI VISION SCORING
Your measurements + reference photos are sent to an AI vision model. It scores 10 face traits and 9 body traits individually, then computes a weighted composite. The AI is calibrated to a realistic bell curve — 5.50 is average, most men score 5-7. It specifically looks for jawline definition, vascularity, muscle separation, symmetry, and overall dating appeal.
4
YOUR RESULTS
You get a detailed trait-by-trait breakdown with individual scores and AI insights, your raw biometric measurements, a composite score, and a tier ranking from SUBHUMAN to GIGACHAD. Share your score card, compete on the global leaderboard, or use the insights to actually improve — the AI tells you exactly what to work on.
TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
Lighting matters most. Face a window. Natural, even light lets the AI see your actual features — shadows create phantom jawlines and false definitions.

Face scans: Hold your phone at eye level, about arm's length. Follow Maxx's prompts — each pose captures different measurement data.

Body scans: Prop your phone 4-5 feet away, leaned against a wall or object. Stand in front of a plain background. Shirtless gets significantly more accurate muscle, body fat, and vascularity readings.

Want the best score? Good lighting + good posture + following every prompt. The AI rewards you for what it can actually see.
