INFO
How It Works
1. Face Mesh Tracking
MediaPipe AI maps 478 landmarks on your face in real-time. Computes jaw angle, canthal tilt, symmetry ratio, facial thirds, and bone structure measurements mathematically.
2. Pose Estimation
33 body landmarks tracked for shoulder width, hip ratio, posture angle, torso proportions. Real measurements from real coordinates.
3. AI Vision Analysis
Photos + landmark measurements sent to Claude AI. Scores each trait against calibrated rubric. Estimates age, height, ethnicity. Generates personalized insights.
Scan Tips
💡 Lighting
Natural side light from a window. Avoid overhead or harsh direct light. Good lighting can swing your score 0.5+ points.
📐 Face Scan Distance
Arm's length. Neutral expression. Eyes open. No smile, no squint.
👕 Body Scan Setup
Shirtless. Prop phone at waist height. Step back 6-8 feet. AI needs your actual proportions.
☀️ Best Time
Morning before eating for body (true baseline). Post-gym for best visual results.
