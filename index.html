THE TECHNOLOGY
THE LOOKSMAXX SCORE™
A biometric attractiveness measurement built on real-time computer vision, machine learning, and a proprietary scoring model that improves with every scan.
THE UNDERLYING TECHNOLOGY
MEDIAPIPE ML PIPELINE
LOOKSMAXX uses Google's MediaPipe machine learning framework running directly on your device. It deploys a face landmarker model that maps 478 three-dimensional coordinates across your facial structure in real time — eyes, jawline, cheekbones, nose bridge, philtrum, orbital bones — at 20 frames per second. For body scans, a separate pose estimation model tracks 33 skeletal landmarks. All detection runs on-device. Nothing is sent to a server until scoring.
PROPRIETARY SCORING ENGINE
Your LooksMaxx Score™ is a blend of two independent systems. The first is the Biometric Structure Score (BSS) — pure mathematics. Each measurement (jaw angle, symmetry ratio, canthal tilt, facial thirds, cheekbone-to-jaw ratio) is mapped to a scoring curve based on published attractiveness research. The BSS is deterministic — same face, same angles, same score. It measures what your bone structure IS, objectively.
The second is the AI Aesthetic Assessment — skin quality, hair, grooming, vibe, and the subjective traits that math can't capture. These two scores are weighted and blended. The BSS anchors your score to reality. The AI adds the human dimension. Neither alone tells the full story.
CONTINUOUS CALIBRATION
The scoring engine tracks the distribution of every scan processed. As the dataset grows, percentile rankings become more statistically significant, score calibration tightens, and the system's accuracy increases. Your score isn't static — it exists relative to an evolving population. Early adopters are establishing the baseline that future scans are measured against.
