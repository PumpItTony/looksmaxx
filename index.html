01
LANDMARK DETECTION
Google MediaPipe runs on-device ML to track facial geometry or body pose in real-time. No data leaves your phone during scanning. The green mesh overlay shows exactly what the AI sees.
02
GUIDED POSE SEQUENCE
The scanner walks you through 7-10 poses — front, profiles, chin tilt, smile for face. Arms out, lat spread, side profiles, flex for body. Each pose captures measurements from a different angle for accuracy.
03
BIOMETRIC COMPUTATION
Raw landmark coordinates are converted into objective measurements: jaw angle, canthal tilt, facial symmetry ratio, shoulder-to-hip ratio, posture angle, and proportional indices. These numbers anchor the scoring.
04
AI SCORING ENGINE
Measurements plus reference photos are analyzed by a vision AI that scores each trait individually. Visual traits (skin, muscle definition) use the photo. Structural traits (jaw, symmetry, frame) use the mathematical measurements. Scores are calibrated against population-level attractiveness research.
